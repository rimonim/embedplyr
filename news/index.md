# Changelog

## embedplyr 0.3.0

- [`predict.embeddings()`](https://rimonim.github.io/embedplyr/reference/predict.embeddings.md)
  deprecated in favor of
  [`emb()`](https://rimonim.github.io/embedplyr/reference/emb.md)
- Improvements to documentation
- JOSS peer review

## embedplyr 0.2.0

- [`align_embeddings()`](https://rimonim.github.io/embedplyr/reference/align_embeddings.md)
  for aligning separately trained embeddings
- [`total_dist()`](https://rimonim.github.io/embedplyr/reference/total_dist.md)
  and
  [`average_sim()`](https://rimonim.github.io/embedplyr/reference/total_dist.md)
  for quantifying differences between groups of embeddings
- Easyloading for
  [HistWords](https://nlp.stanford.edu/projects/histwords/)

## embedplyr 0.1.0

- Initial release.
